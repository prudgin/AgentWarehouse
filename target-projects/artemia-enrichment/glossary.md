# Glossary — 2025 Artemia Enrichment

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Artemia enrichment** — Process of feeding newly-hatched Artemia an emulsion that loads lipid and HUFA into them before they are themselves fed to fish larvae.

**Selco S.presso / Primo Selco S.presso** — Commercial enrichment emulsions. Selco is the standard product; Primo Selco S.presso is the newer variant being trialed in parallel.

**Enrichment tray** — Hatchery tray of Artemia in the enrichment phase. Rows A–D are designated for enriched trays (per `Proposal/Notes 21-10-2025.docx`).

**HUFA** — Highly Unsaturated Fatty Acid — the lipid class enrichment is designed to load (relevant to larval Murray cod brain + eye development).

**Corey** — Hatchery operator receiving the pre-trial enrichment recipe handover (`Proposal/pre-trial artemia enrichment sw - Corey.docx`).

**SW** — Saltwater — the medium Artemia are hatched and enriched in.
