# Glossary — 2024 Bioremediation

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Bioremediation** — Using biological organisms (here: microalgae) to remove or convert pollutants. The strategy this project scopes.

**ANACC** — Australian National Algae Culture Collection (CSIRO). The published strain list is the catalogue of available cultures.

**ANASS** — Australian National Algae Supply Service. The procurement arm — order form and pricing under `Reports/`.

**Aquna report (Elena final)** — Scoping report drafted by Elena. The key narrative document for this project.

**Chlorella** — Single-celled green alga, the leading candidate for in-pond bioremediation. Downstream cultivation work happens in `~/ResearchProjects/2025 Chlorella and PSB Stage 1/`.
