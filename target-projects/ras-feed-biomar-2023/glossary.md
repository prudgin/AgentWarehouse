# Glossary — 2023 RAS Feed Biomar

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**RAS** — Recirculating Aquaculture System — closed-loop tanks with mechanical filtration. The trial's production environment.

**T2 – T29** — Numbered RAS tanks. The trial uses tanks T2 through T29 (two reports split the initial-weights record: T2–T22 and T23–T29).

**T0 / T1** — Trial timepoints: T0 = start of trial (initial weights + initial health), T1 = first follow-up sampling.

**Protein-energy modelling** — Biomar's formulation methodology — balances protein and energy density to match the species' growth curve.

**BSL histo** — Histology results from BSL lab — `Data/BSL histo results/`.

**Aquna / Biomar protocol** — The joint trial protocol; V2 and V5 are revision versions, with V5 being the trial's final operating document.
