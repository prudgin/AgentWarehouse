# Glossary — 2025 Chlorella and PSB Stage 1

Domain vocabulary for the Step 1 combined Chlorella + PSB culture trial. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

## Cultures

**Chlorella vulgaris** — single-celled freshwater green alga. Cultured here in 500 mL flasks scaled to 1 L bottles. Visual/turbidity/cell-count progress markers. Target: contamination-free biomass suitable as enrichment material or pond additive.

**PSB (probiotic suspension / photosynthetic bacteria)** — *Bacillus*-based probiotic suspension. Prepared in 100 L batches in repurposed carboys from nutrient medium and a Bacillus starter culture. Trial-dosed weekly at ≈ 66 L per 11.5 ML pond. (Some literature uses PSB to mean *purple non-sulphur bacteria* — see `Proposal/Photosynthetic bacteria (PSB) - purple non-sulphur bacteria (Rhodopseudomonas Palustris).pdf` — confirm intended usage per trial run.)

**Bacillus starter culture** — seed culture used to inoculate each PSB batch. Startup cost ~$100; media ~$20 per batch.

## Trial setup

**Stanbridge lab area** — small enclosed lab with blackout curtains, LED grow lights, CO₂ cylinder + flowmeter, pressure cooker (used as autoclave), and basic PPE. Shared workspace for both algal and bacterial cultures in Step 1.

**Step 1 / Step 2** — two-phase plan. Step 1 (this project) is establishing the two culture systems. Step 2 will be larger-scale production and downstream feeding / pond-performance trials.

**Pond dose** — current target: 66 L of 100 L PSB batch per 11.5 ML pond, weekly. Subject to adjustment based on pond water-quality response.
