# Glossary — 2025 Hatchery Feed Trial

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Inve** — Hatchery / nursery feed brand from INVE Aquaculture (Belgian). One of the two trial arms.

**Otohime** — Hatchery feed brand from Marubeni Nisshin Feed (Japanese). The other trial arm. O.range Minipellets is one variant family.

**1 mm extruded diet** — Final diet size in the hatchery → nursery transition. Trial endpoint.

**Weaning → 1 mm** — Full lifespan window the trial covers — from larval weaning off live feed through to the first nursery diet at 1 mm pellet size.

**Primo (Inve)** — The trialed Inve product variant (`Articles/010225_INVE Fish Hatchery and Nursery Diets_with Health_TOP Primo.pdf`).
