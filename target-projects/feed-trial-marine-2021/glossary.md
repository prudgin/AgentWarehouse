# Glossary — 2021 Feed Trial Marine vs Non Marine vs LAP

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**LAP** — Land Animal Proteins. One of the feed-formulation arms in the trial — protein source from terrestrial animal by-products rather than marine fishmeal.

**Marine diet** — Feed formulated high in marine-based protein (fishmeal) and oils.

**Hayashikane / Biomar / Ridley** — The three feed brands trialed. Hayashikane is Japanese; Biomar is Danish; Ridley is Australian.

**WP09 / Whitton** — Site names where condition scoring was carried out — WP09 is a specific pond identifier.

**Condition scoring** — Standardised visual fish-condition assessment used at multiple timepoints to track performance under each diet.

**Histology T0** — Baseline histopathology sampling at trial start, used as the comparison reference for end-of-trial gut/liver health changes.
