# Glossary — 2021 Cages Round vs Rectangular

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Round cage** — In-pond cage with circular footprint. One of the two treatments under comparison.

**Rectangular cage** — In-pond cage with rectangular footprint. The other treatment under comparison.

**Pond 3 (Bilbul)** — The trial site — cages were stocked in paired round/rectangular configurations within a single pond.

**DS data** — Recorded data set; `_DS data` suffix is the user's convention for the active trial-data folder in a project.

**Final weight check** — End-of-cycle sampling pass that produces the trial's biomass-and-mortality summary. Two were conducted: April 2023 (interim) and December 2023 (final).

**Nutrisea Pty Ltd** — External research partner. Dr Trent D'Antignana (Director/Principal Scientist) led the trial under contract.
