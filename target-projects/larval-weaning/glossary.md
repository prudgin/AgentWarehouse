# Glossary — 2022 Larval Weaning

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Weaning** — Transition of larvae from live feed (Artemia, rotifers) onto dry diet. The trial's central event.

**Live feed** — Artemia (brine shrimp) and rotifers — the standard pre-weaning feed for Murray cod fry.

**Weaning protocol** — Standardised step-down regime: timing of dry-diet introduction, ratios over each day of the transition. Document in `Proposal/Larval weaning protocol.docx`.

**Small system** — Tank configuration used in the trial. Diagrams in `Proposal/Tank setup diagram.docx`.

**.enl file** — EndNote citation library. `Articles/Larval Weaning.enl` collects references used to design the protocol.
