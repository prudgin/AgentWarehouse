# Glossary — 2022 Deakin Univ

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Trial 22-001** — Internal Deakin-collaboration trial code. Organisation doc in `Proposal/Trial 22-001 Organisation V1.doc`.

**FRDC** — Fisheries Research and Development Corporation — Australian federal R&D co-funder. Application docs in `Proposal/`.

**GUT Health Visual Scoring** — Standardised scoring system used to grade gut histology; criteria in `Proposal/GUT Health Visual Scoring form and criteria MCA.docx`.

**Bulka bag** — Standardised feed-batch container. `Proposal/Deakin Feed Trial - Bulka bag contents.docx` tabulates per-bag batches, dates, and fishmeal percentages.

**SGR / FCR** — Specific Growth Rate / Feed Conversion Ratio — primary performance metrics combined into the Cod Cost modelling deck.

**MP04C2** — Tank/cage identifier whose mortality per day is tracked in `Data/percent Mort per day and unit MP04C2.xlsx`.
