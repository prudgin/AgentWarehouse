# Glossary — 2025 Stanbridge Feed Trial

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Stanbridge site** — MCA freshwater pond site (distinct from Bilbul and Whitton). Site map in `Articles/stanbridge site map.pdf`.

**Feed ration** — Per-day mass of feed delivered to each pond. The single independent variable the trial optimises.

**Static pond** — Pond without continuous flow-through. Standing-water husbandry as opposed to RAS or open-pond configurations.

**Bi reports** — Stream of bi-monthly progress reports separate from the interim/final formal reports. Under `Reports/bi reports/`.

**Erin Bubner / Trent D'Antignana** — Authors of the interim report. External scientific contributors (Nutrisea Pty Ltd).

**Interim report v19.12.25** — Filename versioning: this is the 19 Dec 2025 cut of the interim report. Later versions supersede earlier ones.
