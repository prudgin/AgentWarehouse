# Glossary — 2024 Probiotics Trial

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Epizym PST** — Control probiotic product. Weekly maintenance dose 250 g per pond. Vendor literature in `Articles/epizym-pst3.pdf`.

**Pro-W** — Treatment probiotic product. Weekly dose 324 g per pond — dissolved in 20 L bucket of pond water for 30 min, then spread evenly in paddlewheel flow.

**EcoShock** — Secondary trial product, single-pond. Dose: 1 × 250 g bag + ½ bag dissolved in 10 L water, weekly.

**Sulphide testing (mixed vs unmixed)** — Assay tracking dissolved sulphide as a proxy for pond-bottom waste. Mixed and unmixed bottle preparations are compared. Data: `Data/sulphide testing unmixed vs mixed.xlsx`.

**Mercatus entry** — Each treatment event must be entered into Mercatus at pond level (total g, on the day applied). The trial enforces strict weekly cadence.

**Paddlewheel flow** — The point of application for Pro-W and EcoShock: dissolved product is added in the active paddlewheel flow so it disperses across the pond.
