# Glossary — 2022 CSIRO Selective Breeding

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**MCA004** — Internal R&D project reference for the CSIRO selective breeding program.

**EBV (Estimated Breeding Value)** — Per-fish genetic-merit score derived from pedigree and phenotype data. Drives broodstock selection.

**Phase II** — Second multi-year stage of the breeding program. Phase II update presentation is the May-2024 deliverable in `Reports/`.

**CSIRO** — Commonwealth Scientific and Industrial Research Organisation — Australia's national science agency, the research partner running the genetics work.

**Pedigree** — Recorded parent-offspring relationships across generations. The substrate the EBV pipeline operates on.
