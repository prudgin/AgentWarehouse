# Glossary — 2022 Whitton Feed Trial

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Whitton site** — MCA pond site distinct from Bilbul. WP09 and WP10 are the two specific ponds used in this trial.

**Trent's annotated copy** — `Data/Whitton Feed Trial 2022 June -October TD 22_11_22 Trent.xlsx` — Trent D'Antignana's marked-up copy of the trial sheet (TD = his initials).

**Initial / final condition scoring** — Two reference timepoints (May = initial, October = final) for measuring fish-condition delta over the trial.

**Hayashikane / Biomar / Ridley** — The three feed brands in the panel (same as the 2021 marine-vs-LAP trial — see sibling project `2021 Feed Trial Marine vs Non Marine vs LAP`).

**Modelled biomass** — Computed biomass-at-stocking used as the trial-start baseline. `Data/modelled biomass and density  31-5-22.xlsx`.
