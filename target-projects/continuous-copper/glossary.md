# Glossary — 2023 Continuous Copper

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Chilodonella / chylo** — Common gill / skin protozoan parasite. Cause of recurrent treatment events at MCA — the target the copper regime is meant to suppress.

**Continuous copper** — Low-dose ongoing copper presence in pond water (vs the alternative: episodic shock treatments).

**Mercatus** — MCA's production management / record-keeping system. Treatment events are entered in Mercatus; presence/absence of chylo at health-check is separate.

**Geosmin** — Earthy off-flavour compound, secondary topic on the project meeting agenda (`Proposal/230427_Copper meeting.docx`). Not the primary investigation.

**Round v Rectangular cages** — Sibling trial whose cages provided the retrospective copper-treatment data. See `~/ResearchProjects/2021 Cages Round vs Rectangular/`.
