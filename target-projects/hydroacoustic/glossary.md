# Glossary — 2026 Hydroacoustic Biomass Estimation

Domain vocabulary for the hydroacoustic biomass / behaviour survey project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

## Equipment and vendor

**BioSonics DT-X Extreme** — scientific split-beam echo sounder; the primary instrument for this project. Operated by Infofish Australia under contract.

**Auto Track** — BioSonics' real-time fish-detection-and-counting software, run alongside DT-X. Outputs tracked targets that can be size-graded.

**Infofish Australia** — vendor running the surveys. ~30 yrs in AU recreational fishing & natural resource management; leading operator of BioSonics gear in Australia. Methods statements (V1.5 Jan 2026) in `Proposal/`.

## Surveys

**Biomass survey** — down-looking and side-looking acoustic passes to detect, count, and size-grade fish in a pond. Auto Track produces per-target records; aggregated to biomass estimates.

**Behaviour survey** — movement-pattern survey relevant to husbandry decisions (response to feeding, lighting, etc.). Separate methods statement and analysis workflow.

**Side scan imaging** — supplementary high-resolution imaging of pond bottom and structure, run alongside acoustic survey.

**Pondage mapping** — bathymetric / habitat mapping pass, separate methods statement (`Proposal/SurveyMethods-PondageMapping-2026.docx`).
