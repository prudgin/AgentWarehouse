# Glossary — 2023 Fish Carcass Composition

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Proximate analysis** — Standard four-way decomposition of fish tissue: moisture, crude protein, crude lipid, ash. The base layer of the carcass profile.

**Amino-acid profile** — Per-amino-acid concentration in the carcass — drives target dietary AA spec.

**First Sampling** — The trial's initial sampling pass at Biomar fish (2022-07-29). Subsequent samplings are not yet present.

**DPI NSW Lab** — Department of Primary Industries (NSW) analytical laboratory; quote ref QR-220527-001. Provides proximate + AA + mineral analyses.

**Biomar Carcass Chart** — Vendor-supplied baseline composition chart used as the cross-reference for MCA's data. `Reports/Biomar Carcass Chart_31-8-2022.pptx`.

**Species-specific feed** — End goal: a recipe tuned to Murray cod tissue composition rather than borrowed from another farmed species.
