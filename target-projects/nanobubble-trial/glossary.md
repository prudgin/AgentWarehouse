# Glossary — 2022 Nanobubble Trial

Domain vocabulary for this project. Add new terms here when they first resolve.

**Nanobubble** — Sub-micron-diameter gas bubble (typically oxygen) suspended in water. Stable for long periods; in theory delivers higher net DO than standard aeration.

**Moleaer** — The nanobubble-generator manufacturer/supplier. Vendor docs in `Data/Moleaer/`.

**Trial 1 / Trial 2** — Two sequential phases of the project — first establishing the response in a single pond, then expanding the comparison. Both summarised in `Reports/Final Report _MCA_Nanobubble Trial 1 and 2 _2022_23 (25.10.23).pdf`.

**Whitton site** — Site identifier; `Data/WHITTON EVALUATION O2.xlsx` is the location-specific DO record.

**EOFY expenses** — End-of-financial-year cost summary. `Expenses/Nanobubble expenses EOFY.xlsx`.
